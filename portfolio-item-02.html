eClassroom

Your Academic Companion

eClassroom is an android application developed by me and my classmates in undergrad.

eClassroom is our favorite class companion in our university among our classmates. It provides class routines, class materials (slides, books, etc.), instant notice, classmates' information, and much more.

I was the database designer for this project. I have also contributed to data updates, software testing, design ideas, etc.
